Dorothy Vaughan

  • Birth-Death: September 20, 1910 to November 10, 2008
  • Lived: Lived in Newport News, West Virginia
  • What she did: She was the leader of the West Computers of NASA. She got appointed this position in 1949 She then performed complex computations and analyzed data for aerospace engineers. This was important because the work was essential for the U.S. space program.
  • Impact: She made a big impact by paving the way for black women in STEM. This is because she was the first African-American to receive a promotion at NASA
