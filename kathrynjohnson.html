Katherine Johnson

  • Birth-Death: August 26, 1918 to February 24, 2020
  • Lived: Hampton, West Virginia
  • What she did: In 1969, she calculated trajectories that led the Apollo 11 to the moon She also calculated the trajectory analysis for Alan Shepard's Freedom 7 mission
  • Impact: She had a big impact because she developed calculations that helped the US launch its first astronaut into space in and safely land Apollo 11 on the moon in 1969.
